# Signal Desk Lexicon

**The official terminology registry for Signal Desk.**

This repository catalogs concepts and terminology introduced by
Wilson L Yang in Signal Desk — a publication on capital, technology,
and AI at the global × China intersection.

Each entry in `SIGNAL_DESK_LEXICON.md` establishes:

- **Definition** — precise meaning of the term
- **Coinage event** — when and where the term was first introduced
- **Source URL** — the original Signal Desk article
- **Timestamp chain** — verifiable records of authorship

The Lexicon functions as both an asset ledger and a public
authorship registry.

---

## Current Entries

- **Partition Map** — a 2×2 framework mapping AI assets across
  private/public markets and global/China-connected capital pools
- **The China Discount** — partition-derived structural discount
  on Chinese tech assets in global private markets
- **The Capability–Price Decoupling** — the paired motion of
  converging technical capability and diverging market prices

See `SIGNAL_DESK_LEXICON.md` for full definitions, usage patterns,
and citation tracking.
